About Me

My name is Samiha Amroune;
I passed the final exam for the initiative of One Million Arab Coders in partnership with Udacity
This is my first project in the Scholarship Track Personal Blog; I wanted it to be about raising awareness of the dangers of Breast Cancer, as breast cancer ranks first among the most common types of cancer, globally, regionally, and locally.

Facts:

  1. Breast cancer ranks first among the most common types of cancer, globally, regionally and locally.
  2. Breast cancer is the most common cancer in women.
  3. Breast cancer is more common in women aged 40 years and over.
  4. More than 50% of breast cancer cases are detected in advanced stages, compared to 20% in developed countries. This increases the mortality rate and reduces the chances of recovery, as well as the high cost of treatment.
